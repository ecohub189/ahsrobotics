Building the Future at Ashland High

We're AHS Robotics - a passionate team of students diving into our first year of VEX Robotics competition. Innovation, teamwork, and a whole lot of fun.

Join Our Team Support Us

Who We Are

A group of Ashland High students passionate about robotics, engineering, and problem-solving. We're just starting our VEX journey but we're ambitious and ready to learn.

Our Mission

To foster STEM skills through hands-on robotics while building teamwork, problem-solving abilities, and competitive spirit.

VEX Robotics

We're competing in VEX Robotics competitions, designing and building robots to complete specific challenges.

Team Culture

We value collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning. Everyone contributes and everyone grows.

Location

Based at Ashland High School, 65 E Union St, Ashland, MA 01721. Meetings after school in the STEM lab.
